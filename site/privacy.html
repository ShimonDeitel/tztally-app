Privacy Policy

Timezone Tally is an offline-first app. All of your data — every entry you log — is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data, usage analytics, or identifiers of any kind.

The app uses Apple's StoreKit to process optional in-app purchases. Apple handles all payment information directly; we never see or store your payment details.

Because nothing you enter ever leaves your device, there is no server-side account, no tracking, and no third-party data sharing.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is removed with it.
